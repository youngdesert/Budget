Start Now: Phase 1 (First 30–90 Days)

For entrepreneurs with capital: the goal is to validate demand fast, build a simple offer, and launch. Spend in stages—earn the right to scale.

Step 1 — Pick a Market

Choose a niche where people already spend money.

  • High pain / high urgency problem
  • Clear buyer (not “everyone”)
  • Existing competitors (good sign)

Step 2 — Validate Demand

Before building, confirm real interest.

  • 20–30 customer conversations
  • Landing page + waitlist
  • Pre-sell: deposit / pilot offer

Step 3 — Build the Offer

Make it simple and measurable.

  • Outcome-based promise
  • Clear price + delivery timeline
  • Guarantee or risk reversal (if possible)

Step 4 — Launch (Lean)

Launch with a basic product/service and iterate fast.

  • Simple website + payment
  • Organic distribution (content + outreach)
  • Small paid tests (controlled budget)

Step 5 — Install Systems

These 3 systems separate hobbies from businesses.

  • Sales pipeline + CRM
  • Delivery process + SOPs
  • Finance tracking: margin + cashflow

Capital Strategy

Use capital like fuel, not a lottery ticket.

  • Stage 1: validation + marketing tests
  • Stage 2: inventory/team only after traction
  • Stage 3: scale distribution, not complexity
